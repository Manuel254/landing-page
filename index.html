World wide financial empowerment program is a non profit organisation, empowering people financially so as to help them live more than what their pay cheque offers. The organization was founded by Yannick Moritz and a group of dedicated people form Austria. The mission of world wide financial empowerment is to give financial freedom to all by helping the old, young, disabled, retired and others with our empowerment program.
